Atuel River

The Atuel River, located in the San Rafael department of Mendoza, is renowned for its stunning landscapes and thrilling rapids. Offering a range of difficulty levels, it caters to both families looking for a scenic adventure and experienced rafters seeking a challenge. As you navigate through the breathtaking canyons and witness the spectacular rock formations, you'll be captivated by the natural beauty that surrounds you. The Atuel River promises an unforgettable experience, combining excitement with awe-inspiring scenery.

Tunuyán River

The Tunuyán River, originating from the majestic Andes, offers a thrilling rafting experience with a blend of tranquil sections and exciting rapids. Its diverse landscape, featuring panoramic views of mountains and valleys, makes it a favorite among nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Suitable for both beginners and those with more experience, the Tunuyán River allows you to explore the beauty of Mendoza's wilderness while enjoying an adrenaline-pumping adventure on the water.

Blanco River

The Blanco River, located near Potrerillos in Mendoza, is an ideal destination for those seeking a more tranquil rafting experience. Known for its crystal-clear waters and lush natural surroundings, the Blanco River is perfect for families and beginners who want to enjoy the beauty of the outdoors without the intensity of challenging rapids. This serene river allows for a relaxing adventure, where you can appreciate the stunning landscapes and wildlife while creating lasting memories with your loved ones.
