Cascade Rapids

Client Testimonial

At Cascade Rapids, we are a passionate team of outdoor enthusiasts dedicated to providing unforgettable rafting experiences on Argentina's stunning rivers. Founded by a group of adventure lovers with years of experience in guiding and rafting, we believe in the power of nature to inspire and transform. Our mission is to share our love for the great outdoors while prioritizing safety, fun, and environmental responsibility. Each of our guides is expertly trained, ensuring that every trip is not only thrilling but also safe for adventurers of all skill levels. We are committed to fostering a sense of community among our guests, encouraging teamwork and camaraderie on every journey. Join us as we navigate the breathtaking rapids, explore hidden gems, and create lasting memories in the heart of nature!

History

Cascade Rapids was founded in 1995 by a passionate group of outdoor enthusiasts who recognized the breathtaking potential of Mendoza’s natural landscapes for adventure sports. In 2006, we introduced new activities beyond rafting, including kayaking, canyoning, and zip-lining, making them a comprehensive adventure tourism provider.

We embrace the values of community, and environmental responsibility. Through our dedication to providing unique adventures and our commitment to the local environment, we have created a legacy that continues to inspire adventure seekers from all over the world.
